About this project

Downtown Brooklyn is participating in New York City's Wireless Corridor Initiative to bring information and the power of the web to New Yorkers no matter where they live or work. New Yorkers will be able to connect to free WiFi powered by Fon, NYCEDC, and DBP in outdoor public places, merchants and in residential neighborhoods in and around the Downtown Brooklyn area (bordered by Schermerhorn St, Cadman Plaza West, Flatbush Ave and Tillary St).
